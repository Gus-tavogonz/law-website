Attorney Profile

Migir Ilganayev graduated from the City University of New York, Brooklyn College in 2012 with a B.A degree in Political Science and Philosophy of Law.

Mr. Ilganayev received his Juris Doctorate from New York Law School in 2015. While in law school, Mr. Ilganayev was awarded the Public Service Certificate for his pro bono work at Volunteers of Legal Service and Reality House, Inc., in NYC where Mr. Ilganayev assisted low-income New Yorkers with various legal issues.

During his last year in New York Law School, Mr. Ilganayev was a judicial intern to Supreme Court Justice, Honorable Martin M. Solomon in the Kings County Supreme Court. While interning at the Kings County Supreme Court, Mr. Ilganayev assisted the judge on a number of civil cases and gained a valuable experience and insight into how civil issues are analyzed and decided.

Prior to entering law school, Mr. Ilganayev owned and managed an award winning restaurant in Brooklyn, New York. Through the responsibility of having his own business, he developed the interpersonal and analytical skills needed to be an effective attorney.
